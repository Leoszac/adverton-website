Window Replacement-specific questions

Common questions from window replacement contractors.

Do you serve window replacement + repair, or just full replacement?
Both. Full-replacement keywords ($10K–$40K jobs) and repair keywords (broken seals, locks, glass) are very different conversion paths. We separate them in your ad campaigns and on your website so high-ticket leads aren't competing with low-margin repair calls.
Why a 12-month plan?
Your website is built for your business and transfers to you by month 12 — you own it, at no extra cost. Want to take it and run it yourself down the road? There's a clean exit — just ask.
What's NOT included?
Just the ad spend itself, paid directly to the platforms — Google Ads ($1,000–2,500/month typical for LSA). We manage the campaigns; you control the budget. Everything else (website, GBP, CRM, account manager) is included.

our story

One Phoenix family, three generations of HVAC.

My abuelo started Phoenix Pro out of his pickup truck in 2006. He had two rules: show up on time, and charge what you'd charge your neighbor. Almost twenty years later, those rules still run the company.

Today my dad and I run the family business out of a real shop on 16th Street. We've got 14 trucks, a team of 22, and customers we've been serving since the day we opened. Most of our work still comes from referrals — neighbors telling neighbors.

We're not the biggest HVAC outfit in Phoenix. We're the one your block calls when they want it done right.

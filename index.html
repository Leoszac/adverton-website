Common questions

Straight answers, no fine print.

Why a 12-month plan?
Real results compound over 90+ days — Google rankings, ad performance, and reviews all need time to build. The plan covers building your website and running your full marketing system. Your website is built for your business and transfers to you — you own it, at no extra cost. Want to take it and run it yourself down the road? There's a clean exit — just ask.
Who owns the website?
You do. Your website is built specifically for your business and transfers to your name — you own it, at no extra cost. If you ever leave, your website and your customer data come with you. We keep running the system that brings the jobs — LSA, GBP, hosting, CRM, and support — for as long as you want us.
What's NOT included?
Just the ad spend itself, paid directly to the platforms — Google Ads ($1,000–2,500/month typical for LSA). We manage the campaigns; you control the budget. Everything else (website, GBP, CRM, account manager) is included.
Any surprise costs or upsells later?
No. The bundle has everything a contractor needs to start. We have optional add-ons (extra service-area pages, Meta Ads, Local SEO Pro, AI voice receptionist, etc.) that you can turn on later only if your business grows and asks for them. Zero forced. Zero contract. View the optional add-ons catalog →
Can you manage my Angi or Thumbtack while I transition?
Yes. If you have an active subscription with Angi, Thumbtack, or Networx, we can manage them under our Lead Marketplace Management add-on — dispute bad leads, optimize spend, respond to messages, and progressively shift budget to your owned channels (Google ads, GBP, your website) as they ramp. Most clients are off marketplaces entirely by month 9.
What about my current website?
If you have one, we migrate without losing your Google ranking. Your existing reviews and traffic stay intact.
How fast can you launch?
Typical launch is 2–3 weeks from signed contract to ads live. Week 1: onboarding call + content collection. Weeks 2–3: site build, GBP optimization, ads launch.
What do I need to activate Google Local Services Ads (LSA)?
LSA requires Google's verification of your business credentials before ads can run. To activate, you'll need: (1) a current state license for your trade, (2) general liability insurance certificate (we'll request a copy), (3) a background check for the business owner — Google runs this through its current background-check provider, usually a few business days and at no cost (subject to Google's process), (4) if you have employees, background checks for each technician who will be sent to homes, and (5) verified business address and phone number. We handle the entire enrollment process for you, including coordinating the background check and submitting documents to Google. Once verified, the "Google Guaranteed" badge appears on your LSA listing — a trust signal customers look for.
Do I have to know marketing?
No. That's the whole point. We do the work. You take the calls and run the jobs. One monthly call to review the numbers — that's it.

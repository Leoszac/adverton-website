Plumbing-specific questions

Common questions from plumbing contractors.

Do you handle 24/7 emergency call routing?
Yes — call tracking via CallRail captures every call. If you don't answer, it forwards to your team or to our optional AI Voice Receptionist add-on which books the job into your calendar 24/7.
Can you target commercial plumbing?
The base bundle is built for residential. Commercial requires different sales motions and longer cycles — we can adjust the targeting if that's your focus, but be honest with us in the discovery call so we set the right expectations.
Why a 12-month plan?
The website + setup + 12 months of marketing is valued at $9,588. You pay it in 12 installments of $799. By month 12, the website transfers to your name. There's also a clear buyout option — pay the remaining balance any time and the website transfers immediately.
What’s NOT included in the $799?
Just the ad spend itself, paid directly to the platforms — Google Ads ($1,000–2,500/month typical for LSA). We manage the campaigns; you control the budget. Everything else (website, GBP, reviews, CRM, account manager) is in the $799.
Any surprise costs or upsells later?
No. The $799/mo bundle has everything a contractor needs to start. We have optional add-ons (extra service-area pages, Meta Ads, Local SEO Pro, AI voice receptionist, etc.) that you can turn on later only if your business grows and asks for them. Zero forced. How fast can you launch?
Typical launch is 2–3 weeks from signed contract to ads live. Week 1: onboarding call + content collection. Weeks 2–3: site build, GBP optimization, ads launch.
What do I need to activate Google Local Services Ads (LSA)?
LSA requires Google's verification of your business credentials before ads can run. To activate, you'll need: (1) a current state license for your trade, (2) general liability insurance certificate (we'll request a copy), (3) a background check for the business owner — Google runs this through Pinkerton, takes 3–7 business days, and is free, (4) if you have employees, background checks for each technician who will be sent to homes, and (5) verified business address and phone number. We handle the entire enrollment process for you, including coordinating the background check and submitting documents to Google. Once verified, the "Google Guaranteed" badge appears on your LSA listing — a major trust signal that lifts conversion.

1. Parties

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is between MDS LLC (Delaware), doing business as Adverton ("Adverton", "we"), and the business identified in the Stripe Checkout session that accepts these terms ("Client").

2. Services

Adverton will provide the bundled growth-management package described at adverton.net at the monthly fee shown on the Stripe Checkout page, including: a managed website with hosting, Google Business Profile optimization, reputation/review management, and any add-ons selected at checkout.

3. Term & Renewal

The initial term is twelve (12) months beginning on the date of first successful payment. The Agreement auto-renews for additional 12-month terms unless either party gives written notice at least 90 days before the end of the current term.

4. Fees & Payment

The monthly subscription fee is shown on the Stripe Checkout page at acceptance. Adverton charges via Stripe on the same day each month. Failed payments after 7 days suspend service; after 30 days terminate the Agreement (Client remains liable for the remainder of the 12-month commitment).

5. Website Asset

The Adverton-built website is treated as a $9,588 asset paid through 12 monthly installments included in the subscription. Client may buy out the website at any time after month 6 for the remaining balance plus a buyout fee. Reversion-and-fee language from earlier versions does not apply.

6. Intellectual Property

Client retains ownership of trademarks, content provided to Adverton, and customer data. Adverton retains ownership of all proprietary tools, processes, templates, and code, including the underlying CRM system.

7. Termination

Either party may terminate for material breach with 30 days' written notice if the breach is uncured. Early termination by Client during the 12-month commitment requires payment of the remaining months.

8. Limitation of Liability

Adverton's aggregate liability under this Agreement is capped at the fees paid by Client in the 6 months preceding the claim. Adverton is not liable for consequential, indirect, or lost-profit damages.

9. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by Delaware law, with venue in New Castle County, Delaware. The parties waive jury trial.

10. Acceptance

Client accepts this Agreement by checking the consent box and completing payment in the Stripe Checkout session. Stripe records the timestamp of the acceptance; Adverton stores the acceptance record in clients.tos_consented_at and clients.contract_signed_at for audit purposes.

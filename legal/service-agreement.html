Service Agreement

Effective version: v1.1 · Last updated 2026-05-07

1. Parties

This Service Agreement (“Agreement”) is between MDS LLC (a Delaware limited liability company), doing business as Adverton (“Adverton,” “we,” or “us”), and the business identified in the Stripe Checkout session that accepts these terms (“Client,” “you”).

2. Services

Adverton will deliver the following bundled growth-management services (the “Services”):

2.1 Website Asset (one-time build)

  • A custom-built marketing website of up to five (5) pages, mobile-responsive, with online booking capability.
  • Migration of an existing website (if applicable) without loss of Google search ranking.
  • Domain registration (one) and configuration, business hosting, SSL, and one (1) business email mailbox.

2.2 Recurring Services (monthly)

  • Google Business Profile (GBP) setup, optimization, and monthly maintenance.
  • Google Local Services Ads (LSA) account setup, campaign management, and ongoing optimization within the Client’s defined service area. (LSA media spend is paid directly by Client to Google — see Section 7.)
  • Reputation and review management, including automated review-request workflows after each completed job.
  • Tradio CRM access (online booking, mobile CRM, scheduling, GPS routing, quotes, invoices, payments, automated review requests, appointment reminders). Tradio is provided through a third-party partner — see Section 7.
  • One (1) monthly performance report and one (1) monthly strategy call (up to 30 minutes) with a dedicated account manager.

2.3 Optional Add-Ons

Additional services may be selected at checkout or added during the term at the prices published at adverton.net. The current catalog includes:

  • AI Voice Receptionist — $349/mo
  • Meta Ads (Instagram + Facebook) management — $199/mo
  • Local SEO Pro (citation building, schema markup, content for local landing pages, Bing/Apple Maps optimization) — $399/mo
  • Social Media Posts — $199/mo (1 platform), $349/mo (2 platforms), $499/mo (3 platforms)
  • Yelp setup + management — $149/mo
  • Content updates (website edits + 8 social posts/mo on 1–2 platforms) — $99/mo
  • Call tracking (local tracking number, call recordings, attribution by campaign) — $79/mo
  • Lead Marketplace Management (Angi, Thumbtack, Networx, etc.) — $199/mo (1 platform), $349/mo (2 platforms), $499/mo (3 platforms)
  • Multi-location (separate Google profile + ads per location) — +$199/mo each
  • Extra business email — +$15/mo each
  • Ad-spend management (single bill consolidating Adverton fees + ad-platform spend) — 15% of ad budget

Add-ons are billed separately and are not subject to the bonification structure in Section 4. Adverton may change add-on pricing with thirty (30) days’ written notice; existing add-ons in effect at the time of a price change continue at the original price through the end of the then-current 12-month term.

3. Term & Renewal

The initial term is twelve (12) months beginning on the date Adverton receives Client’s first cleared payment (the “Start Date”). The Agreement auto-renews for additional twelve-month terms on each anniversary unless either party gives the other written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days before the end of the then-current term.

4. Pricing & Bonification

The standard, unbonified value of the bundled Services is set forth in Schedule A (Service Components & Values), attached to and incorporated into this Agreement.

In consideration of Client’s full performance of the 12-month commitment, Adverton grants Client a 100% bonification (waiver) of all recurring service charges set forth in Schedule A. As a result, Client’s total monthly payment is $799 (USD), which is allocated entirely as one of twelve (12) installments toward the Website Asset described in Section 5.

The bonification is a conditional benefit. It applies only while Client is in full and continuing compliance with the 12-month commitment. If Client defaults or terminates early other than for Adverton’s uncured material breach, the bonification is forfeited as provided in Sections 5 and 10, and the unbonified standard service value becomes calculable as a measure of damages.

5. Website Asset, Title & Reversion

5.1 Asset Value & Installments

The website built by Adverton is a fixed asset with a contract value of Nine Thousand Five Hundred Eighty-Eight U.S. Dollars ($9,588), paid by Client through twelve (12) consecutive monthly installments of $799 each. The contract value is the price the parties have agreed for the website and is not contingent on Client’s separate use or non-use of the bonified Services.

5.2 Title Retention

Title to and full ownership of the website (including source code, design files, build templates, content management system access credentials, hosted domain name registration, and the underlying hosting account) shall remain exclusively with Adverton until Adverton has received and cleared all twelve (12) monthly installments. Until that moment, Client holds only a limited, revocable license to use the website for Client’s own business purposes.

5.3 Automatic Transfer Upon Completion

Upon Adverton’s receipt and clearance of the twelfth (12th) installment, title to the website transfers automatically to Client without further action. Within fourteen (14) days thereafter, Adverton will deliver to Client (a) an export of the website code and content in a portable archive (.zip), (b) administrative ownership of the domain name, and (c) a portable export of Client customer records held in Tradio CRM, subject to Section 7.

5.4 Early Buyout

Client may, at any time after the Start Date, elect to accelerate transfer by paying the Remaining Asset Balance, calculated as $9,588 minus all installments cleared to date, in a single non-refundable payment. Upon clearance, transfer occurs as provided in Section 5.3. No additional buyout fee applies.

5.5 Reversion on Default or Early Termination

If Client (a) fails to cure non-payment of any installment within thirty (30) days of its due date, or (b) terminates this Agreement before completing all twelve (12) installments other than for Adverton’s uncured material breach, then, automatically and without further notice:

  • Client’s license to use the website terminates immediately;
  • Title to the website remains with, and reverts in full to, Adverton;
  • Adverton may take the website offline, redirect or transfer back the registered domain, suspend hosted services, and retain all build assets, code, and configurations;
  • The bonification granted in Section 4 is deemed forfeited as to all months remaining in the 12-month term; and
  • Client remains liable, as liquidated damages, for the lesser of (i) the sum of the remaining monthly installments through month 12, or (ii) the unbonified standard value of Services delivered through the date of termination plus the Remaining Asset Balance. Adverton shall elect between (i) and (ii) at its sole discretion.

For clarity: under no circumstance does the website transfer to Client before Adverton has been paid in full, whether through completion of the 12-month installments or through the Early Buyout in Section 5.4.

6. Fees & Payment

The monthly subscription fee is $799 USD, charged via Stripe on the same day each month beginning on the Start Date. Failed payments suspend service after seven (7) days and may trigger termination and reversion under Section 5.5 after thirty (30) days. Add-on fees are charged on the same monthly cycle. All fees exclude sales, use, or value-added taxes, which are Client’s responsibility where applicable.

7. LSA Ad Spend & Third-Party Services

7.1 Google LSA Media Spend

Adverton manages Google Local Services Ads (LSA) campaigns on Client’s behalf. The associated media spend (typically $1,000–$2,500 per month) is paid directly by Client to Google and is not included in the $799 monthly fee. Adverton has no control over Google’s billing, lead pricing, or auction outcomes, and is not responsible for spend incurred under Client’s Google account.

7.2 Tradio CRM (Partner Platform)

Tradio CRM is provided by an independent third-party partner (GrabTradio.com). Access to Tradio is bundled within the Services for the duration of this Agreement, but Adverton does not own or operate the Tradio platform. If Tradio modifies its terms, raises its prices, restricts functionality, or ceases operations, Adverton may, at its option, (a) substitute a comparable CRM, (b) pass through changed costs to Client with thirty (30) days’ notice, or (c) discontinue the CRM component without affecting Client’s other obligations under this Agreement.

7.3 Other Third Parties

Hosting, domain registrar, email provider, and any other infrastructure providers are subject to their own terms. Adverton selects providers in good faith but does not warrant their uptime, security, or continued availability.

8. Customer Data & Migration

Customer records, leads, contacts, and other Client business data entered into Tradio or otherwise provided to Adverton remain the property of Client. Upon completion of the 12-month term and full payment, or upon Early Buyout under Section 5.4, Adverton will provide Client with a one-time portable export (CSV) of Client’s customer data within fourteen (14) days. Client is responsible for maintaining its own backups during the term. Adverton is not liable for data loss caused by third-party platforms outside of Adverton’s reasonable control.

9. Intellectual Property

Client retains ownership of its trademarks, trade names, brand assets, content provided to Adverton, and customer data. Adverton retains exclusive ownership of its proprietary tools, processes, build templates, internal frameworks, dashboards, and any work product not specifically assigned to Client under Section 5. Upon transfer of the website under Section 5.3, Adverton grants Client a perpetual, non-exclusive license to use the delivered website code and content for Client’s own business; no rights are granted in Adverton’s underlying templates or framework code.

10. Termination

10.1 Termination for Material Breach

Either party may terminate this Agreement for the other party’s uncured material breach upon thirty (30) days’ written notice specifying the breach. If the breach is cured within the notice period, termination does not take effect.

10.2 Early Termination by Client

Termination by Client during the 12-month commitment, other than for Adverton’s uncured material breach, triggers reversion and damages as set forth in Section 5.5. There is no right to terminate for convenience during the initial term.

10.3 Effect of Termination

Sections 5 (Website Asset, Title & Reversion), 9 (Intellectual Property), 11 (Limitation of Liability), 12 (Dispute Resolution), and any accrued payment obligations survive termination.

11. Limitation of Liability

In no event shall Adverton be liable for consequential, indirect, incidental, special, exemplary, or lost-profit damages, even if advised of the possibility. Adverton makes no guarantee as to lead volume, conversion rates, or business outcomes.
